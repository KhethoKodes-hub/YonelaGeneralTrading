Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and our services.

For general inquiries, email is often the most efficient method. For urgent matters or to discuss specific properties, calling our direct line at 076 529 1405 is recommended. You can also use our contact form for non-urgent queries, and we'll respond within 24 business hours.

Our regular business hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. For emergency maintenance issues, we have a 24/7 emergency line available to current tenants.

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that the appropriate team member is available to assist you with your specific needs. You can call ahead or use our contact form to request an appointment.

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. Emergency maintenance requests are addressed immediately through our emergency line. During business hours, phone calls are typically answered live or returned within a few hours if we're temporarily unavailable.

When inquiring about a property, please include:
  • Your name and contact information
  • The property address or reference number if known
  • Your timeframe for needing the property
  • Specific requirements (number of bedrooms, amenities, etc.)
  • Any special needs or questions you may have
This information helps us provide you with the most accurate and helpful response.
