About Yonela General Services

Discover our journey, leadership, and commitment to excellence in property management and accommodation services.

Our Story

Yonela General Services (PTY) Ltd is a proudly South African, 100% Black Woman-owned private company established in 2013, strategically based in Olifantshoek, Northern Cape.

Our Foundation

Founded in September 2013, we emerged from recognizing the growing need for quality accommodation services in the Northern Cape region. Our strategic location in Olifantshoek positions us perfectly to serve the accommodation demands driven by mining activities and economic growth in the province.

As a private company, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional residential property investment services while maintaining our core commitment to community development and empowerment.

Our Purpose

We specialize in residential property investment with a focus on property acquisition, development, and comprehensive management services. Our core business addresses the rising accommodation needs in the Olifantshoek area and surrounding regions.

The general mining activities and economic growth in the Northern Cape province attracts a great number of people who need quality accommodation, and we are strategically positioned to serve this essential need with excellence.

Our Vision

To provide the best customer-focused property rental services, property development and acquisition so that we are the most preferred brand in the South African property market.

Our Mission

Our mission is to exceed expectations of tenants and relevant stakeholders by continuously providing comfortable, affordable and secure accommodation with consistently reliable customer service.
